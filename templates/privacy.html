Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect basic information such as your name and email address. As you use Spendly, we also store the expense records you create, including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service — displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and personalising your experience. We do not use your financial data for advertising or sell it to any third party.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third-party services. We may use infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the app, but these providers are contractually prohibited from accessing or using your data for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the app's support channel. We are committed to addressing any privacy-related concerns promptly.
